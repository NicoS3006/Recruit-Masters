Why we do, what we do.
A Fresh Take on Recruitment

We’re changing the way people see recruiters. No pushy sales tactics, no empty promises, just genuine, people-first hiring that puts trust and integrity at the heart of everything we do.

We believe recruitment should feel like a partnership, not a transaction. Whether you’re a business looking for the right fit or a candidate searching for a career move that actually excites you, we’re here to make the process seamless, honest, and even enjoyable.

Real People, Real Results

We know the recruitment industry has a reputation, and we’re here to rewrite it. Our team is made up of down-to-earth professionals who genuinely care about finding the right match, not just filling roles for the sake of it.

We take the time to understand your needs, your goals, and what truly makes a difference to you. We don’t just connect people with jobs, we help build careers, teams, and futures with integrity and purpose.

The Founder

Selena

Hello, I’m Selena Vitale, Founder and Principal Consultant at Recruit Masters. With over 15 years of specialised experience in recruitment, I bring a deep understanding and genuine passion for connecting businesses with exceptional talent.

My career has been dedicated to the manufacturing, logistics and warehousing sectors, where I have successfully placed candidates ranging from food processing staff to pick packers, forklift drivers and voice pickers, contributing to Brisbane’s thriving workforce. What differentiates Recruit Masters is a commitment to building lasting partnerships.

I take pride in working closely with each client, gaining a thorough understanding of their business objectives, team culture and long-term goals. This personalised approach allows me to source candidates who not only meet the role requirements but seamlessly integrate into the organisation, adding lasting value.




Selena Vitale, Director / Co-Founder

The Founder

Apelu

I am a proud Samoan professional, originally from New Zealand and now based in Brisbane, Australia. My career spans several key industries, including warehousing, cold storage, earth-moving equipment, logistics, retail and healthcare.

I have had the privilege of contributing to well-regarded organisations such as Michael Hill Jewellers, Komatsu Australia, Tyre Power, Dairy Farmers, Lion Nathan and Queensland Health. Through roles ranging from operations and logistics coordination to team development and leadership, I have developed a comprehensive skill set and a strong focus on operational excellence and people management.

I am now focused on the next chapter of my career with the launch of our own recruitment firm. I am committed to delivering tailored recruitment solutions by fostering relationships built on trust, integrity and long-term value. My approach is grounded, pragmatic and people-focused, ensuring both clients and candidates achieve lasting success.




Apelu Vitale, Co-Founder
